### About
#### Description
BudgetSIM is a simple budget simulator which allows young children (intended for ages 10-14) to get a general idea of how the United States must manage its budget. Different, life-like events are given to the player and they must decided how to change their budget in order to successfully combat the problem. After each event, three metrics will be displayed: Foreign Threat Safety, Emergency Services, and Public Happiness

#### Context
This game/simulator was created for the Congressional App Challenge of 2019. It will be submitted to Representative David Joyce of Congressional District OH-14.
